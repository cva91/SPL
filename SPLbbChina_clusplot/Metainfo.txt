
Name of Quantlet: SPLbbChina_clusplot

Published in:     SPL

Description:      Shows a scatterplot where each point is included into a cluster.
                  Different clusters are visualized by a colored ellipse encircling
                  their particular points. Cities are clustered by (centered) population density
		  and (centered) share of secondary industry. 

Keywords:         cluster analysis, clusplot, cluster

Author :          Bjoern Bokelmann

Datafiles:         AAP_PM_database_May2014.xls, economic_indicators.xls

Input:		  Data of economic factors for chinese cities

Output:		  Visualization of clusters according to economic factors.
